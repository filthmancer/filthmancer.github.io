How to Play

Guess if each of the 7 prompts are real or fake (made up).

The number of fakes varies. They could all be fake!

Enjoy new puzzle topics daily and strive for a high score.

Hit the share button to let your pals know how you did!

Themes

Backstory

Once upon a time, Studio Folly's own Darcy Smith joined a conservationist on his dad's country radio show. To spice things up, he came up with a game called Real Bird Fake Bird, where they had to guess if Australian birds were real or totally made up. When we realized the game had wings (and didn't have to stop at birds), we gathered some friends and turned it into a cute daily game to add to your rotation.
